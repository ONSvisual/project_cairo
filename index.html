The Digital Content Team

Our team focuses on improving the understanding and engagement with ONS analysis.

We offer a range of services in data visualisation, journalism, graphic design, content design and social media. These services are broadly,

Improve: Making analysis more understandable and interesting for any user

  • Advice on making your writing clearer and in plain English
  • Advice and support on charts that clearly communicate the main points behind your analysis
  • Advice on structure, publication formats and meeting the word count limits
  • Summarising content clearly, for high profile products such as the Insights tool or Economy roundup
  • Supplementing publications with additional content, for example content for social media.

Collaborate: Initiating projects to create citizen-focused content

  • Contributing ideas for analysis that will be of interest to a citizen audience
  • Partnering with analytical teams to produce this content

Innovate: Inventing and developing new products primarily for citizen users

These may include elements of:

  • Scrollytelling
  • Robo-journalism (or semi-automated journalism)
  • Custom mapping

Our sister team is Publishing, which handles website publication, organisation, and essential publishing operations.

Some projects will fall across more than one team. We will ensure that the people best suited to your content are working on it.
